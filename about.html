About

Early years

As a northern Wisconsin native, I spent most of my childhood years outside in the woods, or on my grandparent's farm just down the road. I remember helping with baling hay, bringing the cow feeder to the pasture, or just causing general mayhem. As kids we spent a lot of time biking and fishing in nearby lakes.

As I grew older I excelled in sports as a three sport athlete, although my real passion was basketball, leading our team to two state appearances, including the first in our school's history.

I was also a bit of a nerd growing up. I had been an avid gamer since my parents first got me the original Nintendo Entertainment System, and I've never stopped playing games. Through the years I graduated from Nintendo, to SNES, N64, Xbox, Playstation and now PC.

Upon graduating from Clayton, I decided to move to Madison, WI and pursue a degree from the University of Wisconsin. In 2007 I graduated with a Bachelor of Arts with a major of History.
